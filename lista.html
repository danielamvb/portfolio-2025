Graphic Design Identity

For my local school’s student association, I created a custom variation of their logo for sweatshirts and t-shirts as part of their campaign. The design aimed to reflect their identity while keeping it fresh and wearable. Additionally, I worked on the layout and design of their monthly school newspaper, focusing on clear typography, engaging visuals, and a structured layout to enhance readability and appeal, while also keeping it simple.
